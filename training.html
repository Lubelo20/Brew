Training

Our comprehensive training programs are designed to equip you with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to succeed in the coffee industry. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to advance your expertise, we have a program for you.

Basic Barista Course

R1,800 - R2,500

What You'll Learn:

  • Coffee basics and bean knowledge
  • Espresso machine operation
  • Milk steaming and latte art basics
  • Customer service fundamentals
  • Hygiene and safety standards
  • Basic coffee brewing techniques

Duration: 2-3 days

Ideal For: Complete beginners and aspiring baristas

Certification: Basic Barista Certificate

Professional Barista Course

R3,500 - R4,500

What You'll Learn:

  • Advanced espresso techniques
  • Latte art mastery
  • Coffee tasting and quality control
  • Menu development
  • Equipment maintenance
  • Business basics for coffee shops
  • Inventory management

Duration: 5-7 days

Ideal For: Those planning to work professionally or start their own business

Certification: Professional Barista Certificate

Advanced Barista Course

R7,000 - R8,500

What You'll Learn:

  • Coffee roasting fundamentals
  • Advanced brewing methods
  • Competition preparation
  • Staff training techniques
  • Business management and finance
  • Marketing and branding
  • Supply chain management
  • Quality assurance systems

Duration: 10-14 days

Ideal For: Experienced baristas and entrepreneurs scaling their businesses

Certification: Advanced Barista & Business Certificate
